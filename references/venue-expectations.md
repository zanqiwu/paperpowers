# Venue Expectations

Calibrate ambition and evidence to the likely venue family.

## Top-Tier ML Or AI Conference

Usually expects:
- clear and specific novelty
- strong baselines
- thorough experiments
- careful analysis of limitations

Weaknesses punished heavily:
- unclear delta from prior work
- incomplete comparisons
- claims broader than evidence

## Domain Conference Or Workshop

May accept:
- narrower novelty
- applied contribution
- focused but useful empirical evidence

Still requires:
- honest positioning
- fair baselines
- a clear reason the work matters

## Journal

Usually expects:
- stronger completeness
- fuller related work
- more ablation, robustness, or analysis
- clearer reproducibility details

## Systems Or Engineering Venue

If efficiency, deployment, or tooling is the main contribution, make sure the paper includes:
- realistic workload or user setting
- resource metrics
- comparisons against relevant systems baselines
- discussion of tradeoffs, not just wins
